# 持节 (Chijie)

Product name for this Chrome extension fork (formerly Nanobrowser / briefly 奕枢).

## Etymology

**持节** — classical office of the envoy: one who **holds the credentials (节)** and acts with the sovereign's mandate.

- 《汉书》等：使节出疆，持节专对。
- Product mapping: the user is the principal; the agent holds temporary authority to operate the browser; **approval** is the 节 for irreversible external commits; **verified completion** is reporting back with evidence.

| English short | **Chijie** |

## One-liner

Personal **browser action agent**: multi-step web tasks in daily Chrome, approve external commits, verified completion only.
